Enzymes eating away your organic waste

The Foodbuster™ is a digester, it functions according to a fully biological process and does so by the means of enzymes that eat and digest your organic remains/waste. The enzyme formula is patented and tailor made for your specific food diet, and it is done so by our teams of specialised bioengineers. The Foodbuster™ as well as the enzyme formula is engineered in such a way that if one does not input the designed diet into the machine, the enzymes will self reproduce and basically never need refilling.

Intake of the of the organic waste

The User will input the organic food waste diet such as engineered with his/her enzyme formula. The Foodbuster™ works just like a normal stomach, too much of one specific type of food might cause a similar reaction to drinking too much soup...

Decomposition of the organic waste

Once plugged into the socket, the process will start, the machine will increase its core temperature to the optimal temperature for the enzymes to work and reproduce themselves. The process takes about 24 hours and will reduce the volume of your waste by up to 90%. Throughout the process the Foodbuster™ will only release filtered and odourless gaz as well as a bit of water. Once the process over, you will be left with a brown powder that we call Green Droppings™

How our machines work

No piping no drilling no drain pump; the machine is plug and play and only requires a socket as well as passive extraction (access to the outside). The machine only rejects an odourless gas, a bit of water and has a low energy consumption.

1.

Plug

Find a space for the Foodbuster™ to operate in. It may be installed in your dishwashing area.

2.

Put your food rests into the Foodbuster

Dispose of the food rests according to your tailor made enzyme formula diet

3.

Collect the Green Droppings™

24 hours later, collect your Green Droppings™ and store them for collection.

4.

Collection

Our certified partners will regularly come and collect your Green Droppings™ via carbon neutral means as part of the solution that you have opted to in partnering with us.
