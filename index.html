Foodbuster - A food waste solution company

Do you have any idea how much you spend on bin collection?

Did you know that...

Enzymes eating away your organic waste

The Green Pig™ is a digester, it functions according to a fully biological process and does so by the means of enzymes that eat and digest your organic remains/waste. The enzyme formula is patented and tailor made for your specific food diet, and it is done so by our teams of specialised bioengineers. The Green Pig™ as well as the enzyme formula is engineered in such a way that if one does not input the designed diet into the machine, the enzymes will self reproduce and basically never need refilling.

Intake of the of the organic waste

The User will input the organic food waste diet such as engineered with his/her enzyme formula. The Green Pig™ works just like a normal stomach, too much of one specific type of food might cause a similar reaction to drinking too much soup...

Decomposition of the organic waste

Once plugged into the socket, the process will start, the machine will increase its core temperature to the optimal temperature for the enzymes to work and reproduce themselves. The process takes about 24 hours and will reduce the volume of your waste by up to 90%. Throughout the process the Green Pig™ will only release filtered and odourless gaz as well as a bit of water. Once the process over, you will be left with a brown powder that we call Green Droppings™

Collect and post treatment

Wasteam is about a project of long term partnership as well as the forging of a circuit of jobs centred around the common ideal of reducing waste and working towards a cleaner, renewable future. We therefore work with certified partners that will insure a regular carbon neutral collection of your transformed waste, Green Droppings™; and other partners that will refine the Green Droppings™ into Fertiliser.
